
TASK 4 DISCUSSION ANSWERS

Question (a): Does coherent detection work for both?
Answer: Yes. Coherent detection works perfectly for both DSB-LC and DSB-SC. 
Unlike the envelope detector, it recovers the sign (phase) of the message correctly.

Question (b): Which has better quality?
Answer: Coherent detection has better quality. The envelope detector introduces 
small "ripples" from the carrier and can have diagonal clipping, whereas the 
coherent detector mathematically recovers the exact message signal after filtering.

Question (c): Main advantage of coherent detection?
Answer: The main advantage is that it can demodulate DSB-SC signals (which are 
power-efficient) and it works regardless of the modulation index (no distortion if mu > 1).

Question (d): Main disadvantage (implementation challenge)?
Answer: The implementation challenge is **Phase and Frequency Synchronization**. 
The receiver must generate a local carrier that is exactly matched to the 
incoming carrier. If there is a small frequency or phase error, the output will 
experience "fading" or severe distortion.
